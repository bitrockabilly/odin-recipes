Chimichanga Recipe

Description

A Chimichanga is easy to make and tastes so delicious!
There are few ingredients needed and you can have dinner ready in a few minutes.

Chimichangas are a crispy burrito filled with beans and cheese and any meat if you so desire.

Ingredients

  • Burrido size flour tortillas
  • Refried beans
  • Grated Chedder Cheese
  • (optional) Meat of your choice
  • Olive Oil
  • Sour Cream
  • Iceburg Lettuce
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Your favorite hot sauce

Steps

  1. Lay tortilla flat
  2. Put refried beans on tortilla (keep toward center of tortilla)
  3. Put cheese on beans
  4. Roll tortilla (fold left and right, then top and bottom)
  5. Heat frying pan at medium heat with olive oil covering entire bottom of pan
  6. Carefully put burrido into oil in pan and let cook about 2 minutes on each side until golden brown
  7. (best to use tongs to place and remove burrido from pan)
  8. Let burrito cool on paper towel to absord left over oil
  9. Chop lettuce and tomatoes to your desired size
  10. Plate burrido and top with sour cream, chopped tomatoes and chopped lettuce
  11. Add hot sauce if you so desire
  12. Chow down! You will be back for seconds.
