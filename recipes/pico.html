Pico De Gallo Recipe

Description

A good Pico de Gallo is easy to make and will impress your friends and family.

Beware, everyone will always want you to make this for them at every event.

Ingredients

  • Ripe Roma Tomatoes
  • Ripe Beefcake Tomatoes
  • Fresh Cilantro
  • Sea Salt
  • Fresh Ground Pepper
  • Ground Ceyanne pepper
  • Ground Chili Powder
  • Whole White Onion
  • Fresh Bulb of Garlic
  • Whole Red Onion
  • Fresh Green Onion
  • Whole Lime
  • Whole Lemon
  • Fresh Whole Jalepeňo Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut tomatoe ends off and dice tomatoes and put into large bowl.
  2. Chop garlic bulbs or use garlic press into bowl with tomatoes
  3. Chop white, red, and green onions and add to bowl
  4. Chop Jalapeňo into small pieces and add to bowl. (Small amount for flavor, more for spicy heat.)
  5. Add salt, ground pepper, chili powder, ceyanne pepper to taste.
  6. Cut lime and lemon in half then squeeze entire lime and entire lemon into bowl. Throw into bowl after squeeze.
  7. Chop cilantro and add to bowl
  8. Stir everything together.
  9. The longer it sits the more the flavors come out.
  10. Grab some chips or make a dish to add Pico to and chow down!
