Pomodoro Sauce and Pasta Recipe

Description

A good Pomodoro sauce is simple with few ingredients.

When done right it will taste better than any store bought sause and be much healthier for you.

Ingredients

  • Roma Tomatoes
  • Fresh Basil leaves (directly from a basil plant is best)
  • Sea Salt
  • Fresh ground pepper
  • Virgin (Cold Pressed) Olive Oil
  • Fresh Bulb of Garlic

Steps

  1. Cut tomatoe ends off and cut tomatoe into 4 pieces
  2. Chop garlic bulbs or use garlic press
  3. Fill cooking pan with olive oil. Line entir bottom of pan. Set to low heat.
  4. Put garlic in pan. Make sure not to burn garlic but let cook for about 2 minutes
  5. Put chopped tomatoes into pan
  6. Use masher and mash the tomatoes in pan while cooking. Turn up heat to medium
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste
  8. Let simmer for about 15 minutes, ocassionally stirring
  9. Boil and prepare whatever pasta you desire cooked al dente. Fusilli noodles go great.
  10. Take basil leaves off plant and put whole leaves into sauce
  11. Let simmer for no more than 5 minutes once basil is added
  12. Pour over your pasta
  13. Grate fresh parmezan cheese on top if desired
  14. Eat to your hearts content!
